About
Mavin Global is a company founded and run by millennials to serve millennials.

Mavin Global Company is a for-profit corporation, founded in 2010, that provides technology-enabled products, programs, and consulting services intended to empower the way millennials live, work, think, play and act. Through extensive market research and testing, Mavin Global seeks distinction as a global thought-leader on the Centennial generation.

With offices located in Asia, Africa, Europe, North America and South America, Mavin Global thinks globally in all aspects of its operations.
